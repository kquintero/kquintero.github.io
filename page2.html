My Name is Kristopher Quintero,

 I am a beginner designer with a basic skill set in multiple applications ranging from Adobe Photoshop to ROBOTC. I started learning all my skills since I was twelve years old staring middle school to the present day which adds up to five years of continuous learning. I started off with ROBOTC and coded for two years until the end of middle school. I wrote around twelve codes in those two years. I also took computer classes in which I learned the basics of Adobe Photoshop, Microsoft Office and website design. I also joined a S.T.E.M program (Science, Technology, Engineering, Math) and the United States Naval Sea Cadet Corps, a Navy program for young kids to learn what it’s like to be in the military. After middle school I went to Coral Reef Senior High for their engineering academy. In my freshman year I was introduced to AutoCAD and learned the alphabet of lines and drafting and some basic design principals. I designed simple tools, floorplans and entire rooms in 2D and 3D using AutoCAD. I ended freshman year of high school and then transferred to Miami Arts Studio @ Zelda Glazer for their Tech Production Magnet for my final years of high school (Present day). At MAS I have learned the basics of Photoshop, Premiere Pro, Lightroom, Microsoft Word and Mobirise. As I continue my high school years I hope to even further educate myself in the world of technology.
